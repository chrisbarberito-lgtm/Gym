Meal Plan
Moderate cut. Protein anchors ~215g; carbs cycle by day. Weigh your food.
Training day
215P · 160C · 55F
~1,975 cal
Rest day
215P · 120C · 55F
~1,835 cal
Meal 1 — Pre-workout every day
  • Banana — 90 g
  • Coffee + Chobani creamer — ~50 g (measured)
  • Creatine — 5 g
Meal 2 — Post-workout
  • Ground chicken 93–96% — 200 g
  • Egg whites — 250 g
  • Whole eggs — 2
  • Jasmine rice, cooked — 120 g rest 80 g
  • Hot sauce / Tapatio — free
  • Swap: 96% beef 170 g or shrimp 200 g
Meal 3 — Lunch
  • Ground chicken — 200 g
  • Sweet potato, cooked — 180 g rest 120 g
  • Green beans — 150 g
  • Olive oil — 8 g
  • Sugar-free sauce — up to 20 g
Meal 4 — Snack pick one
  • A: Greek yogurt 250 g + blueberries 70 g + whey 20 g
  • B: Whey 40 g + rice cakes 1–2 + almond butter 16 g train days
Meal 5 — Dinner lower-carb to tighten the waist
  • Ground chicken — 200 g
  • Jasmine rice, cooked — 100 g rest 60 g
  • Green beans — 150 g
  • Olive oil — 8 g
Meal 6 — Night sweet stays in on purpose — kills cravings
  • A: Greek yogurt 200 g + cocoa 8 g + blueberries 50 g + sweetener
  • B: Chocolate whey 30 g + almond milk 150–200 ml + ice, blended
Weekly flex meal
  • One flex meal / week — enjoy it, then straight back to your next scheduled meal.
  • Sushi · steak dinner · shrimp boil · fajitas (skip chips & queso)
Hydration & extras
  • Water — 100+ oz/day
  • Creatine — 5 g daily
  • Free condiments: Tapatio, mustard, hot sauce, salsa, lemon, garlic, herbs, sugar-free syrup, zero-cal seasonings
